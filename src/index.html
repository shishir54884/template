    So, how does it work?

    Step 1

    Find a scooter nearby

    Use the map in the app to find your closest scooter. You can also book it 40 minutes in advance.

    Step 2

    Scan QR code to unlock

    Scan the QR code on the scooter using your smartphone camera to unlock it and start your ride.

    Step 3

    Enjoy your ride

    Follow traffic rules and ride safely to your destination. Remember to wear a helmet!

    Step 4

    Park and end your ride

    Park the scooter in a designated area and end your ride through the app. Your account will be charged automatically.
